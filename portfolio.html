Localvore – Mobile Application

Localvore is a mobile application designed to boost local food and store sales throughout a specific region based on the users location. When solving the problem of finding fresh local food, I discovered there was not an app that showcases users trusted stores to source their local goods, all while promoting the local businesses. This app connects users to these local stores with a touch of a button.
Users can sign up through social media by linking their Facebook, Instagram or Twitter accounts and or through a one time sign-up feature. The cost of the mobile application is free due to the paid advertisements local store owners can put on the app. The application is consisted of a mix of store owners and local buyer who consume these products (hence the name 'Localvore') that post images of their products or purchased items. The long scrolling page was emulated from Instagram giving users a long list of images that they could view. All pages are based on the users location, giving them specific stores they can source their desired products.
Each store can sign up for free to promote their business on the application and have the option of placing their products at the top of the page for a cost. When conducting a survey at Smorgasboard in Brooklyn, I found out that 8 out of 10 users would find this application useful on a daily basis. Whether it was figuring out what to have for lunch, sourcing fresh local produce or finding vintage furniture for their brand new studio apartment, Localvore would be an option for users to find that special product.

VAST

I have a very close connection to the outdoors. I can place a finger on almost every state down the East Coast of the United States (and Alaska for the West Coast) and tell you where I have been and what I did. My friends and family are all about the outdoors and I thought that I should help them out before they stepped foot on their journey. I created an outdoor gear company that connects users to brands and products that they will need for their trip. The mobile application connects users to areas that they want to discover and what products they should take with them based on experts. The application also gives users reviews, pictures and tips from experts. The products vary from carabiners, shoes, pants to canoes. The main goal of this project was to reduce the number of websites/applications that users use to figure out gear to bring with them on trips.
