I am currently a Graphic Designer whose been working in Manhattan for over under a year now. I graduated the State University of Farmingdale State College with my Bachelors of Technology in Visual Communications in May 2015. During my last year of college I worked for EGC Group in Melville, NY in the Craft Beverage Department. What's the Craft Beverage Department you ask? BEER. I specialized in creating beer brands throughout the United States from Yakima Valley in Washington State to the East Coast of Long Island. I currently work for a company called Hodes who specializes in Employer Branding for companies such as, The Home Depot, Dick Sporting Goods, TIAA, Honeywell and so much more. I work in the Creative and Production Departments and on a daily basis I pump out work for our amazing clients. I recently took a class at General Assembly for Front End Web Development. I can finally say I coded my first website using HTML, CSS and Javascript...you're looking at it right now.
