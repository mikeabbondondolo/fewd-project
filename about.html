I am currently a Graphic Designer and Junior Web Developer whose been working in Manhattan for two years. I graduated the State University of Farmingdale with a Bachelors of Technology in Visual Communications in May 2015. During my last year of college, I worked for EGC Group in Melville, NY in the Craft Beverage Department. What's the Craft Beverage Department you ask? BEER. I specialized in creating beer brands throughout the United States from Yakima Valley in Washington State to the East Coast of Long Island. I "hopped" into many different opportunities after this.

I work for a company called Hodes, which is a part of Symphony Talent and I specialize in Employer Branding for companies such as, The Home Depot, Dicks Sporting Goods, TIAA, Honeywell and many more. I work in the Creative and Production Departments and on a daily basis I work on pieces for our amazing clients including web banners,miscellaneous collateral and UI/UX for websites. I recently took a Front End Web Development class at General Assembly becuse I wanted to expand my knowledge in digital design. I can finally say I coded my first website using HTML, CSS and Javascript. In fact, you're looking at it right now.
